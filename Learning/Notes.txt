What to Look For: Waveforms
The primary way to debug hardware is by looking at Waveforms (Logic Analyzers). These are graphs where:
The X-axis is Time.
The Y-axis shows the State (0 or 1) of every wire.

The Four States of a Wire
In a simulator, a wire isn't just 0 or 1. It can be in four states:
0: Logic Low (Ground).
1: Logic High (Power).
x: Unknown/Undefined. This usually means you forgot to give a wire an initial value. If you see "Red" on a waveform, something is wrong!
z: High Impedance. This means the wire is disconnected (floating).
